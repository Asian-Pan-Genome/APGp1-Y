## APGp1 (Asian Pan-Genome phase1) Y chromosome project
This page demonstrates the workflow and codes of the major analyse in the manuscript, titled "Origin and structural evolution of the complex genomic regions of human Y chromosome". The assemblies and raw sequencing data for all Y chromosomes generated in this study are part of the APGp1 project and have been deposited in the National Genomics Data Center (https://ngdc.cncb.ac.cn) under BioProject accession PRJCA030428. These data are available to the scientific community for general research through a controlled-access process. Detailed information is provided in the Data Availability section of the APGp1 flagship manuscript (Wu, D., Yang, C., Chen, Q., Suo, M., Zhou, F., Liu, A., Yu, D., Nie, L., Yang, T., Sun, Y., et al. (2026). Hidden Genetic Diversity in 320 Nearly- complete East Asian Pangenome Assemblies. UnderReview).
