#### The majority of 160 Y-chromosome assemblies were derived from the APGp1 (Asian Pan-Genome phase1, https://github.com/Asian-Pan-Genome/APGp1) project(Wu et al., 2026). To ensure the high integrity of the primary Y assemblies, we manually examined all potential mis-assemblies, including abnormally short PAR1 (pseudoautosomal region 1) (3/160), misplacement of PAR2 fragments (12/160), and severely shortened or incorrectly haplotype-assigned heterochromatic regions (Yq12; 15/160) (Supplementary Table 3).
